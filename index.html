Characters In Noli Me Tangere


Crisostomo Ibarra		 He is the main character and the son of Rafael Ibarra, friend of Don Santiago.

Maria Clara		 She is described to be beautiful and proper. She is the love interest of Crisostomo Ibarra.

Padre Damaso		 He is a Fransiscan Priest. He is a very loud and arrogant person. He thinks that he knows everything

Don Santiago		 He is a kind person. He is rich and is close to those who are powerful.

Donya Victorina		 Donya Victorina is a friend of Don Santiago. She wanted to be friends with those who were poweful. Her husband is Don Tiburcio.
